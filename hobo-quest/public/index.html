MMORPG

HoboGame

A full open-world survival MMORPG running right in your browser. Explore a vast procedurally generated 512×512 tile world, gather resources, craft equipment, build bases, and fight monsters — or other players.

Gather & Craft — Chop trees, mine ores, smelt bars, and craft swords, armor, potions, and legendary equipment.
Open-World PvP — Attack anyone, anywhere. Raid bases, steal loot, or make allies. It's kill or be killed.
Base Building — Build walls, doors, furnaces, storage chests, and sleeping bags to protect your loot.
Dungeons & Bosses — Enter procedural dungeons filled with monsters, traps, and epic boss fights.

Canvas

Pixel Canvas

A massive 512×512 collaborative pixel art canvas. Every pixel is a tile you can claim and paint. Create art together, compete for territory, and watch the canvas evolve in real-time.

Place & Paint — Choose from a 24-color palette and place pixels on a shared canvas visible to everyone.
Real-Time Collaboration — See other players' cursors and tiles appear live via WebSocket.
Territory & Leaderboards — Compete for the most tiles placed. Dominate regions of the canvas.
Zoom & Pan — Smooth infinite zoom from pixel-level detail to the full canvas overview.

What's Inside
Everything Runs in Your Browser
No downloads. No plugins. Just open the tab and play. Canvas-rendered, WebSocket-powered, and designed for low-latency multiplayer.

Real-Time Combat

Attack monsters and players with melee and ranged weapons. HP, armor, damage calculations — all server-authoritative.

Fishing

Cast your line in rivers and lakes. Catch fish for cooking XP, sell them for gold, or use bait for rare catches.

Mining & Smelting

Mine copper, iron, gold, and diamond ore. Smelt bars at your furnace and craft powerful gear.

Woodcutting

Chop trees for logs. Build walls, craft tools, and fuel your furnace. Higher-tier trees yield rarer wood.

Crafting System

50+ recipes spanning tools, weapons, armor, potions, and building materials. Discover recipes as you level up.

Base Building

Place walls, doors, furnaces, chests, and sleeping bags. Protect your loot from raiders — or raid others.

Dungeons

Enter procedurally generated dungeons with multiple floors, monster encounters, treasure chests, and boss fights.

Leaderboards

Global rankings for every skill, total level, PvP kills, dungeon clears, and canvas tile placement.

Daily Quests

Fresh objectives every day. Complete quests for bonus XP, gold, and rare item rewards.

Player Trading

Trade items and gold with other players. Buy rare drops, sell your surplus, build your fortune.

Progression
7 Skills to Master
Every action earns XP. Level up to unlock new areas, equipment tiers, recipes, and abilities. Every skill has its own leaderboard.
